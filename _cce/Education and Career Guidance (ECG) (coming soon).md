#### <u>What is Education and Career Guidance?</u>
Education and Career Guidance (ECG) assists students in discovering their sense of purpose in life. By fostering self-awareness and encouraging self-directed learning, ECG supports students in cultivating a growth mindset, adaptability, and resilience. This enables them to seize future opportunities and recognise the importance of diverse careers.

##### <u>The Goals of ECG are to support students in:</u>
* <b><u>Discovering purpose</u></b> - Who am I?<br>
Nurture students' self-awareness to support them in discovering how they can meaningfully play a part in their community.
* <b><u>Exploring opportunities</u></b> - Where do I want to go?<br>
	Develop students' self-directedness and confidence to explore and leverage education and career opportunities while respecting the value of all occupations.
* <b><u>Staying relevant</u></b> - How do I get there?<br>
Build students' adaptability and resilience to embrace the need for lifelong learning.

<img style="width:45%" src="/images/ECG_Goals.jpg"> 

ECG lessons are integrated into the FTGP (Form-Teacher Guidance Period) in schools to help students understand their interests, abilities, and career aspirations. These lessons use activities and resources like the [MySkillsFuture portal](https://www.myskillsfuture.gov.sg/content/student/en/primary.html)  to guide students in making informed decisions about their education and future career paths. 


##### <u>What ECG lessons cover</u>
* <b>Self-discovery</b>: Helping students become aware of their strengths, interests and passions.
* <b>Education and career exploration</b>: Teaching about different jobs, industries and educational pathways.
* <b>Decision-making skills</b>: Developing the ability to make informed choices about their secondary school options and future goals.
* <b>Future-readiness</b>: Fostering resilience, adaptability and a lifelong learning disposition. 

##### <u>How ECG lessons are delivered</u>
* <b>Integrated into FTGP</b>: ECG is a core component of the FTGP curriculum.
* <b>Activities</b>: Lessons include classroom activities and role-playing
* <b>Digital resources</b>: Students use online portals like MySkillsFuture to explore their interests and learn about different careers.
* <b>School events</b>: JYPS organises hands-on experiences such as ECG Circles, ECG LJs and Secondary School Fair to supplement and complement our students’ development to be Future-Ready earners and Leaders of Characters. 

Visit [Overview of Education and Career Guidance](https://www.moe.gov.sg/education-in-sg/our-programmes/education-and-career-guidance/overview)) to learn more about Education and Career Guidance (ECG) and the resources available to support students in their journey from school to further education or work.
