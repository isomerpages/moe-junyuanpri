#### What is Education and Career Guidance?
Education and Career Guidance (ECG) assists students in discovering their sense of purpose in life. By fostering self-awareness and encouraging self-directed learning, ECG supports students in cultivating a growth mindset, adaptability, and resilience. This enables them to seize future opportunities and recognise the importance of diverse careers.

##### The Goals of ECG are to support students in:
* <b><u>Discovering purpose</u></b> - Who am I?<br>
Nurture students' self-awareness to support them in discovering how they can meaningfully play a part in their community.
* <b><u>Exploring opportunities</u></b> - Where do I want to go?<br>
	Develop students' self-directedness and confidence to explore and leverage education and career opportunities while respecting the value of all occupations.
* <b><u>Staying relevant</u></b> - How do I get there?<br>
Build students' adaptability and resilience to embrace the need for lifelong learning.
 
ECG lessons are integrated into the FTGP (Form-Teacher Guidance Period) in schools to help students understand their interests, abilities, and career aspirations. These lessons use activities and resources like the MySkillsFuture portal (insert this link - https://www.myskillsfuture.gov.sg/content/student/en/primary.html) to guide students in making informed decisions about their education and future career paths. 


What ECG lessons cover
•	Self-discovery: Helping students become aware of their strengths, interests and passions.
•	Education and career exploration: Teaching about different jobs, industries and educational pathways.
•	Decision-making skills: Developing the ability to make informed choices about their secondary school options and future goals.
•	Future-readiness: Fostering resilience, adaptability and a lifelong learning disposition. 

How ECG lessons are delivered
•	Integrated into FTGP: ECG is a core component of the FTGP curriculum.
•	Activities: Lessons include classroom activities and role-playing
•	Digital resources: Students use online portals like MySkillsFuture to explore their interests and learn about different careers.
•	School events: JYPS organises hands-on experiences such as ECG Circles, ECG LJs and Secondary School Fair to supplement and complement our students’ development to be Future-Ready earners and Leaders of Characters.
